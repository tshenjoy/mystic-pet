# Desktop Pet App — Idea Bucket

Core constraint: pet IS the tool, not just decoration. Privacy-safe. Works across jobs. Useful daily.

---

## Round 1: Behavior-Based Pet

### Accountability Pet
Pet reacts to work behavior patterns — no explicit input needed.
- High typing + low window-switching → focused → pet happy/energetic
- Rapid window-switching + low typing → scattered → pet restless, sighs
- Long session no break → pet droopy, tired
- Consistent daily schedule → pet grows / gains traits over weeks
- Signals used: keyboard activity rate, mouse pattern, window-switch frequency, idle time, session length, time of day

### Anti-Distraction Guardian
Detects "distraction signature" and reacts with emotional friction (not hard blocking).
- Distraction signature: low typing + high window-switching + low CPU
- Pet response: taps foot, holds tiny sign, covers eyes, sighs
- No site blocking — soft social pressure from a creature you feel guilty disappointing
- Differentiator vs existing blockers: emotional friction, not hard wall

## Round 2: Pet as Tool

### Clipboard Catcher
Pet "catches" everything you copy — sits in corner with a little bag/inventory.
- Click to browse clipboard history
- Drag item out to paste
- Exists as boring utilities (Clipy, Ditto) — never as a pet
- Metaphor fits perfectly: pet literally holds things for you
- Universal need, zero job-specific knowledge required

### Quick Capture Pet
Click pet → type thought/todo/question → done. No app switch needed.
- Pet "absorbs" the note
- End of day: pet spits out everything you told it
- Interaction model: "telling the pet" vs opening an app
- Zero privacy concerns, universally useful

## Round 3: Vibes-Based / Gen Z

### Tarot Pet (Combo: Mystic Companion)
Two-mode pet — idle personality + ritual ceremony.

**Idle mode:** pet randomly whispers throughout day
- Delulu affirmations ("Mercury cooked for you today")
- Cursed thoughts ("are clouds just sky's regrets")
- Lore drops ("pet's grandmother was a lighthouse")
- Mix of pre-written library + LLM-generated content

**Ritual mode:** click pet to start ceremony
1. Curtain/candle animation — vibe shift
2. Pet asks "what brings you, traveler?" (or daily auto-pull)
3. Card shuffle animation. User picks 1 / 3 / Celtic cross
4. Cards flip. Pet reads via LLM (uses cards + question + pet's voice)
5. Closing line. Curtain falls. Idle pet returns

**Why it works:**
- Ceremony = screenshot moment = TikTok bait
- Idle whispers = parasocial bond builds daily
- LLM = infinite unique content
- Vague-girl-coded = no accuracy pressure, just vibes
- Universal, no job knowledge needed

**Open design questions:**
- Card art: classic tarot vs custom decks (skin monetization)
- LLM privacy disclosure: own it with humor
- Frequency sliders for chatty vs quiet pet
- Pet voice consistency between idle + ceremony
- Free tier (library-only idle, limited readings) vs paid (unlimited + premium decks)
- Extensible engine: oracle cards, runes, dice oracle, tea leaves

## Cute Detail Library (Reusable Mechanics)

Small mechanics that can embellish any pet concept. Mostly contextual mimicry + ambient presence.

### Co-Breathing
Pet visibly inhales/exhales at calm pace. Always there. On stress signature (rapid clicks/typing bursts), pet slows breath as invitation. No nag, no popup. Pure co-regulation by example.

### Contextual Mimicry (Body Double)
Pet mirrors your context in tiny delightful ways:
- You type → pet types on tiny keyboard
- You plug in headphones → pet pulls out matching headset
- You enter password field → pet covers eyes politely
- You open file explorer → pet opens tiny file cabinet
- You go fullscreen video → pet grabs popcorn
- You stop activity → pet stops too (silent body-double)
- Late night → pet yawns, rubs eyes
- Long focus session → pet stretches when you stretch

### Screen-Share Awareness
Pet detects screen share / Zoom call → hides off-screen instantly. Reappears when share ends with note: "I waited." Privacy intelligence = pet you can keep on a corporate laptop without shame.

### Eye Contact
Pet's eyes track cursor wherever it goes. Blinks occasionally. Sometimes looks away to give space. Ambient companionship with zero words.

### "Missed You" Returns
Pet reacts to login / return-from-idle. Bigger absence = bigger reaction (3-day weekend = tackle-hug cursor). Fills the "no one welcomes remote workers back" gap.

### Witness Mode
Pet sees you all week, says nothing. Once weekly: ONE single observation ("I saw you Tuesday. you handled it."). Scarcity = impact.

### Hold / Squeeze Interaction
Drag pet with cursor. Click-hold = soft squeak. Hold 30s = pet sleeps in cursor. Desktop fidget toy.

### Slow Beat
Pet takes deliberate beat before any reaction. Stretches, yawns mid-task. Intentional friction against instant-gratification UX.
